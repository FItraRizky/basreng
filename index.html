Our Story

Delicious snacks with a rich heritage

Traditional Recipes, Modern Taste

Founded in 2010, Mae Snack began as a small family business with a passion for creating delicious, high-quality snacks using traditional recipes passed down through generations.

Today, we combine these time-honored methods with modern production techniques to bring you the very best in snack foods, while maintaining our commitment to quality ingredients and authentic flavors.
